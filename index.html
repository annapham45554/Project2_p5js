Bamboo Growth Simulator

Project Description

Bamboo Growth The "Bamboo Growth Simulator" is an interactive animation project where users can click to make bamboo grow on the screen, with the bamboo gradually changing color from dark green to light green to show its growth. Moving the mouse changes the background from bright daytime to dark nighttime, with a sun or moon appearing accordingly. A fun way to explore interactive animations while simulating the growth of bamboo and the transition from day to night.


Instructions

  1. Click anywhere on the canvas for the bamboo to grow
  2. Keep clicking until the bamboo stops growing
  3. Move the mouse along either the x-axis or y-axis to change the background color
  4. Continue moving the mouse until the moon disappears
  5. Refresh the page to restart
